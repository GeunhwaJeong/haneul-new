A Haneul wallet stores the keys that prove ownership of your onchain assets and signs the transactions you submit to the network. This section covers how wallets work, the wallet types available on Haneul (including Slush, self-custodial wallets, and zkLogin wallets), how to integrate wallets into your app, and how HaneulLink connects your wallet across apps.
