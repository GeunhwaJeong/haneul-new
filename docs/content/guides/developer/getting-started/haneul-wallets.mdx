Unlike the CLI option used in [Obtain a Haneul Address](/guides/developer/getting-started/get-address), wallets connected to the Haneul network provide visual workflows that might be preferable to some users. 

The following Chrome extension wallets integrate with your browser and have a UI workflow that walks you through the process of creating an address. Consult documentation for your chosen extension for the exact process of obtaining an address.

:::info

Links to these extensions are provided for your convenience. Their inclusion is not an endorsement, recommendation, or approval of any kind. Perform your own due diligence before making a decision on what extension to use.

:::

- [Slush — A Haneul wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/haneul-wallet/opcgpfmipidbgpenhmajoajpbobppdil): Formerly known as Haneul Wallet, Slush is a wallet from the same team that built Haneul - Haneul Labs.
- [Haneulet | Haneul Wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/haneulet-haneul-wallet/khpkpbbcccdmmclmpigdgddabeilkdpd): The Haneulet wallet for everyone, built on the Haneul blockchain. 
- [Glass wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/glass-wallet-haneul-wallet/loinekcabhlmhjjbocijdoimmejangoa): Glass Wallet is a non-custodial wallet that allows you to control your coins, keys, and NFT securely.
- [Martian Aptos & Haneul Wallet Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/martian-aptos-haneul-wallet/efbglgofoippbgcjepnhiblaibcnclgk): Martian is a self-custodial crypto wallet for Aptos and Haneul.
- [Surf Wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/surf-wallet/emeeapjkbcbpbpgaagfchmcgglmebnen): Surf Wallet is a platform that allows for convenient and secure transfer of tokens and collection of NFTs on the blockchain.
- Visit the [chrome web store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/search/haneul%20wallet) for a complete list of available Haneul wallet extensions.
