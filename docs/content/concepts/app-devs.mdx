## Object Model

The basic unit of storage in Haneul is the object. In contrast to many other blockchains where storage is centered around accounts containing key-value stores, Haneul's storage is centered around objects addressable on-chain by unique IDs. The topics in this section examine the model for objects on Haneul.

Go to [Object Model](./object-model.mdx).

## Transactions

Transactions define the history of activity on a blockchain. On Haneul, you can develop complex programmable transaction blocks that perform several transaction commands in a single execution. You can also sponsor transactions for your smart contract users to streamline onboarding. The topics in this section explore transactions on Haneul at a conceptual level.  

Go to [Transactions](./transactions.mdx).
